Saving Paper

According to data from the Global Forest Resource Assessment roughly 80,000 to 160,000 trees are cut down each day around the world with a significant percentage being used in the paper industry.

Saving Paper Means Saving Nature

The major impact of the constant deforestation is the change in global climatic patterns. Apart from deforestation, the paper manufacturing industry also contributes to air pollution and water pollution. In the United States, paper industries accounted for roughly 20% of the air pollution in 2015. Data indicates that the production of 1 ton of paper contaminates nearly 20,000 gallons of water.
